📐 O Padrão — He is / She is / They are
📊 Estrutura Visual
EstruturaExemploContração
He is + nomeHe is Carlos.He's Carlos.
She is + profissãoShe is the sales coordinator.She's the coordinator.
They are + cargoThey are our top team.They're our top team.
He is from + localHe is from Brasília.He's from Brasília.
She is + estadoShe is in a meeting.She's in a meeting.
They are + sentimentoThey are excited to meet you.They're excited.

🔑 He is = homem / She is = mulher / They are = 2 ou mais pessoas. Não muda o verbo!

💬 At the Office — Conversas
📌 The Situation — A new client visits your company
Conversation 1 — He is
Client:"Hello! Who is he?"
You:"He is Carlos. He is the director. He is from Brasília."
Client:"Is he available today?"
You:"Yes, he is! He is in the office right now. He is excited to meet you!"
Conversation 2 — She is
Client:"And who is she?"
You:"She is Ana. She is the sales coordinator. She is from São Paulo."
Client:"Is she available now?"
You:"No, she isn't. She is in a meeting. She is very experienced!"
Conversation 3 — They are
Client:"And who are they?"
You:"They are Carlos and Ana. They are our top team."
Client:"Are they available for a meeting?"
You:"Yes, they are! They are excited to meet you."

⚠️ As 3 Regras do Dia
❌ Negativa
  • He is not = He isn't
  • She is not = She isn't
  • They are not = They aren't
❓ Pergunta
  • Is he available?
  • Is she in a meeting?
  • Are they engineers?
✅ Short Answers
Is he busy? → Yes, he is.Is she here? → No, she isn't. Are they ready? → Yes, they are.Is he free? → No, he isn't.
